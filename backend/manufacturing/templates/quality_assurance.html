Improve Product Quality and Reduce Defects

The Quality Assurance module helps manufacturers track product inspection, defect history, rejection reasons, corrective actions and approval status. It connects production, inventory and customer delivery with strict quality control.

Quality Inspection Workflow

01

Receive

Receive material or finished product for inspection.

02

Inspect

Check dimensions, visual quality and manufacturing standards.

03

Record

Store inspection results, readings and defect details.

04

Approve

Approve, reject or send product for rework.

Defect Category Monitoring

Dimensional Error

Parts not matching tolerance or drawing dimensions.

Surface Defect

Scratches, cracks, finishing issues and visual defects.

Assembly Issue

Incorrect fitting, missing components or alignment problems.

Material Failure

Wrong material, poor strength or supplier quality issue.

NCR MANAGEMENT

Non-Conformance Report Tracking

When a product fails quality inspection, the ERP creates a Non-Conformance Report. This helps the quality team identify the problem, assign corrective action and prevent repeated defects.

  • ✔ Defect root cause analysis
  • ✔ Corrective action assignment
  • ✔ Supplier quality issue tracking
  • ✔ Rework and rejection decision
  • ✔ Quality approval history
